Holiday Sangria

The Ultimate Holiday Sangria

Holiday Sangria is filled with citrus, pomegranate, pear and cinnamon for one irresistible cocktail! Find out my secret method to making the BEST sangria recipe! You’ll be the star of the holiday party with this easy cocktail recipe!

Ingredients

  1. .17 (750 milliliter) bottle Rioja red wine
  2. ½ tablespoon and ½ teaspoon brandy
  3. ½ (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers orange-flavored liqueur (such as Cointreau)
  4. .17 large orange
  5. .17 large grapefruit
  6. .17 large lemon
  7. 1 clove pomander
  8. ¼ cup lemon seltzer water

Steps

  • Pour Rioja into a large pitcher. Add brandy and orange liqueur. Cut orange, grapefruit, and lemon into wedges. Squeeze juice into the pitcher. Toss in fruit with rinds. Refrigerate until flavors combine, 8 hours to overnight.
